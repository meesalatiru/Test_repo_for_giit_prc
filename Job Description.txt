Job Description:

Infrastructure Engineer – Linux 

The Mid-level Linux Systems Administrator will be responsible for all aspects of Linux and Unix Server based systems. Incident management, effective troubleshooting skills, and problem management with root cause resolution are critical requirements of a Mid/Senior Systems Administrator. Additionally, a Mid/Senior Systems Administrator needs to be a leader for the team; mentoring team members to ensure success.  The position assumes full accountability along with the other members of the Operations Support team for delivery of services that meet all business requirements in a timely fashion. The position also collaborates with the Application Services and other IT team to provide a stable and secure server infrastructure.
 

Primary Job Responsibilities

Responsible for day-to-day Linux/Unix administration
Responsible for maintaining access controls and system security
Participates in change control process for Epiq infrastructure changes
Responsible for vendor escalations as required
Ensures systems uptime and stability
Very good in troubleshooting and production support for Linux
Ensures process & procedures are followed and documentation is completed when new services are deployed
Ensures Business Unit needs are met within agreed upon timelines
Supports Application Services team as needed
Continually seeks ways to improve services including automation of manual processes
Primarily this role expectation to handle Linux support task from India late hours shift

Additional International Team Responsibilities

Unix/Linux administration which include but not limited

 scripting, automation, log rotation
Linux networking, DHCP, DNS
Permissions and ACLs
Epiq application installs, upgrades and best practices
SQL support  - daily support on writing queries, custom queries, assistance in Relativity mobile deployments if needed only
